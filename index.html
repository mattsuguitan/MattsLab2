University of Washington Basketball

Retired Jerseys

In the world of college athletics, the University of Washington as of late has been most recognizable for their football program after reaching the College Football Playoffs in 2016 and 2024. However, deeply rooted in Seattle is the basketball culture that was once set by legends whose jerseys hang in the rafters of Alaska Airlines Arena. Players such as Isaiah Thomas, Brandon Roy and Kelsey Plum have retired numbers at the University of Washington for their accomplishments, accolades, and impact they had on the University and the city of Seattle.
